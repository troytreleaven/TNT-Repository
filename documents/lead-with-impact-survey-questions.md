# Lead with Impact - Survey Questions
## Ready to copy into Google Forms

---

### Form Title: **Lead with Impact - Program Feedback Survey**

### Form Description:
Thank you for participating in the Lead with Impact program! Your feedback helps us continuously improve the experience for future leaders. This survey takes approximately 5-7 minutes to complete.

---

## SECTION 1: Onboarding Experience

**Section Description:** Please rate your experience with the onboarding process (forms, DISC assessment, and coaching call).

### Question 1 (Required)
**Type:** Linear Scale (1-5)
**Title:** How satisfied were you with the overall onboarding experience?
- 1 = Very Dissatisfied
- 5 = Very Satisfied

### Question 2 (Required)
**Type:** Linear Scale (1-5)
**Title:** The onboarding coaching call helped clarify my goals and expectations for the program.
- 1 = Strongly Disagree
- 5 = Strongly Agree

### Question 3 (Optional)
**Type:** Short Answer
**Title:** What could we improve about the onboarding process?

---

## SECTION 2: 3-Day Leadership Boot Camp

**Section Description:** Please share your feedback on the intensive 3-day boot camp experience.

### Question 4 (Required)
**Type:** Linear Scale (1-5)
**Title:** How satisfied were you with the 3-day Leadership Boot Camp?
- 1 = Very Dissatisfied
- 5 = Very Satisfied

### Question 5 (Required)
**Type:** Linear Scale (1-5)
**Title:** The instructors were knowledgeable and engaging.
- 1 = Strongly Disagree
- 5 = Strongly Agree

### Question 6 (Required)
**Type:** Linear Scale (1-5)
**Title:** The content was relevant to my leadership role and challenges.
- 1 = Strongly Disagree
- 5 = Strongly Agree

### Question 7 (Optional)
**Type:** Paragraph
**Title:** What was the most valuable takeaway from the boot camp?

---

## SECTION 3: Leadership Labs (Weekly Sessions)

**Section Description:** Please rate your experience with the ongoing Leadership Labs.

### Question 8 (Required)

### Question 9 (Required)

**Section Description:** Final questions about your complete Lead with Impact journey.

### Question 10 (Required)
**Type:** Linear Scale (0-10)

- 0 = Not at all likely
- 10 = Extremely likely

### Question 11 (Optional)

**Title:** What has been the biggest impact of this program on your leadership?

### Question 12 (Optional)

## Form Settings Recommendptions:

✅ **Collect email addresses** - So you can follow up
✅ **Limit to 1 response** - Prevent duplicates  
✅ **Show progress bar** - 5 sections, respondents see their progress
✅ **Enable response receipts** - Send confirmation to respondents
✅ **Customize theme** - Use Dale Carnegie colors (red #D71920, black #1a1a2e)

## Response Collection:
- Responses go to Google Sheets automatically
- Set up email notifications for new responses
- Consider adding a thank you message with your contact info
