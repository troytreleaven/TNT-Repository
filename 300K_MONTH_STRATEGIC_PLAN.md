### Sales Vocabulary (From Existing SOP)

**Opening Approach:**
- Relationship-first using shared history/connections
- "Lift the hood" philosophy - diagnose before prescribe

**Positioning:**
- Present options (evening vs Lead with Impact), not one-size-fits-all
- Use analogies (ship turning, oscillating fan)

**Objection Handling:**
- Budget concerns: Payment plans, ROI proof
- Technical-only focus: Leadership is transferable
- Team size: Small teams benefit most

**Closing:**
- Specific dates, collaborative framing
- Reference past participants from their company
- Offer group discounts (3-person: 10% off, 7-person: pay for 6)
