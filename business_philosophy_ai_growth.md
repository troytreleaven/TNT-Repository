# Business Growth Philosophy — Inspired by David Ondrej

## Core Philosophy
> "With AI, you can build anything. Any idea, any app, any startup. It's just a few prompts away."

## The New Society Framework

### 1. **AI as the Primary Builder**
- AI agents can build, automate, and scale
- No programming experience needed
- 30 minutes per day is enough to make progress
- Leverage Claude Code, Cursor, and AI agents

### 2. **Build Systems, Not Just Apps**
- Create reusable agents and automations
- Document prompts that work
- Build once, use forever

### 3. **Customer-First Automation**
- Structured system for acquiring paid customers
- Automate outreach, follow-ups, and sales
- Let AI handle repetitive tasks

### 4. **Continuous Learning**
- Weekly community calls
- Step-by-step blueprints
- Learn by building
