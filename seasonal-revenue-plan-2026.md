## 3. JULY STRATEGY - THE MITIGATION PLAN

### Why July is Slow

| Factor | Impact | Data Point |
|--------|--------|------------|
| **Vacation Season** | SEVERE | 20-30% of professionals away |
| **Family Priorities** | HIGH | Summer kids out of school |
| **L&D Budget Cycle** | HIGH | Q3 = planning season, not buying |
| **Decision Lag** | MODERATE | Summer = "let's wait until fall" |
| **Event Fatigue** | MODERATE | Competes with summer activities |

**Result:** B2B training sees 25-40% revenue decline in July

### July Mitigation Strategy

#### A. Corporate Deals (Non-Seasonal)

**Target industries with different cycles:**

| Industry | Why Non-Seasonal | Approach |
|----------|------------------|----------|
| **Healthcare** | Year-round hiring, regulated training | Compliance training, leadership |
| **Financial Services** | Ceaseless training requirements | Certifications, client relations |
| **Technology** | Continuous upskilling | Soft skills for technical teams |
| **Manufacturing** | Q3 maintenance shutdowns | Training during slow periods |

**Tactics:**
1. **June push:** Close 3-5 corporate contracts before July
2. **Multi-year deals:** Lock in annual training packages
3. **Q3 planning:** L&D leaders planning for Q4 - position for delivery in fall

#### B. Virtual Training Alternatives

**Virtual DCC Option:**
- 8-week evening format works well virtually
- No venue costs = higher margins
- Attendance不受 vacation as much (evening, home)
- **Price:** $1,995 (vs. $2,495 in-person)

**Virtual Boot Camp:**
- Half-day sessions over 2 weeks
- Reduced venue overhead
- **Price:** $795 (vs. $1,295 in-person)

#### C. July Revenue Tactics

| Tactic | Revenue Potential | Feasibility |
|--------|-------------------|-------------|
| Corporate contract close (June carryover) | $30,000 - $50,000 | HIGH |
| Virtual DCC cohort | $15,000 - $25,000 | MEDIUM |
| One-on-one coaching packages | $10,000 - $20,000 | MEDIUM |
| Online course/pre-work products | $5,000 - $10,000 | LOW |
| **Total July Target** | **$80,000 - $100,000** | **AGGRESSIVE** |

### July Survival Checklist

- [ ] Identify 10 corporate prospects for June close
- [ ] Develop virtual DCC curriculum (June pilot)
- [ ] Create "Summer Leadership" coaching package
- [ ] Book instructor coverage for minimal in-person delivery
- [ ] Set expectations with team: July = maintenance mode
