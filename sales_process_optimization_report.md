## 3. LEAD RESPONSE TIME

### The Hard Truth

| Response Time | Close Rate Impact |
|--------------|-------------------|
| < 5 minutes | 8-10% |
| 1 hour | 5-7% |
| 24 hours | 2-3% |
| 48+ hours | < 1% |

**Impact of delay:** Every hour you wait after a lead expresses interest, your close probability drops by ~15%. A lead contacted within 5 minutes is **8x more likely to convert** than one contacted after 24 hours.

### Recommended Response Protocol

1. **Immediate (< 5 min):** Auto-responder email with calendar link
2. **Within 1 hour:** Personal outreach (text + email)
3. **Within 24 hours:** If no response, second touch via different channel
4. **Day 3:** Third touch with value-add content (case study)
5. **Day 7:** Final attempt, then move to "nurture" list

### Automation Requirement

**Implement immediately:**
- Auto-responder with Calendly link on all inbound
- SMS notification to rep's phone for hot leads
- Task creation in CRM for follow-up

## 4. PROPOSAL EFFECTIVENESS

### Current Close Rate: ~20-30%

This is below industry benchmark (3545%). Here's- how to improve:

### Proposal Improvement Checklist

**Before Sending:**
- [ ] Confirmed budget authority (who approves?)
- [ ] Identified all decision-makers
- [ ] Aligned solution to 3+ specific pain points discovered
- [ ] Included relevant case study (same industry/size)
- [ ] Clear next-step with specific date

**Proposal Structure:**
- Executive summary (1 page max)
- Problem statement (their words)
- Solution overview
- Investment (3 options: Good/Better/Best)
- Timeline
- Testimonials/case studies
- Guarantee or pilot option

### The Close Rate Improvement Formula

| Current | Target | Method |
|---------|--------|--------|
| 20-30% | 40-45% | Add value-add touchpoints |
| 40-45% | 55-60% | Multiple decision-maker inclusion |

**Key insight:** Proposals with 3 options close 35% better than single-option. Always present Good/Better/Best.

## 5. SALES VOCABULARY

### What Language Converts ✅

Based on the SALES_SOP analysis, these phrases work:

**Trust-Building:**
- "It's great if it's a collaboration between you and I"
- "I trust you - this is your area of specialty"
- "I'm always a proponent of..."

**Discovery:**
- "Is that fair?" (confirmation)
- "Help me understand..."
- "What's the vision for...?"

**Empathy:**
- "It's all a bit of a blur, isn't it?"
- "That's a big challenge"

### The Pitch (60-Second Version)

For Influence Boot Camp:
```
"We help shared services and operations leaders 
develop the influence skills they need to drive 
organizational change. Unlike technical training, 
we focus on the human skills - communication, 
leadership, relationship building - that separate 
good managers from great leaders.

Our clients include [relevant examples]. The 
program typically runs [format - 3-day intensive 
or weekly evening]. What's your biggest challenge 
right now in developing your team?"
```

### Objection Handling

| Objection | Response Pattern |
|-----------|------------------|
| "No budget" | "Let's look at this as an investment - what's the cost of NOT developing your people?" |
| "Already have training" | "Most organizations do. The question is whether it's moving the needle on the specific challenges you're facing." |
| "Send just one" | "We always recommend teams. They get collaboration and support each other. Plus, there's a price break at 3+ people." |
| "Need to think about it" | "Absolutely. What specific aspects do you need to think through? Let me make sure I address them." |
| "Not the right time" | "I understand. What's driving that timing? And what's the best way to reconnect when it makes more sense?" |
