# GO-TO-MARKET STRATEGY: 2-WEEK LAUNCH FEASIBILITY ANALYSIS

## Executive Summary: BRUTAL REALITY CHECK

**VERDICT: CONDITIONAL GO** — A 2-week launch is possible for a pilot event, but filling seats requires pre-existing assets (email list, LinkedIn network, or partnerships). Without these, realistic timeline is **4-6 weeks minimum**.

---

## 1. CHANNEL STRATEGY: FASTEST TO REVENUE

### RANKED BY SPEED TO FIRST SALE:

| Rank | Channel | Speed | Why |
|------|---------|-------|-----|
| 1 | **Existing Database Email** | 24-48 hrs | Immediate access, no ad spend, highest intent |
| 2 | **LinkedIn Direct Outreach** | 3-7 days | Personal network, direct access, no ad approval |
| 3 | **Partner Co-Promotion** | 5-10 days | Borrowed audience, instant credibility |
| 4 | **LinkedIn Ads** | 7-14 days | Ad approval, learning phase, optimization |
| 5 | **Chambers/Associations** | 2-4 weeks | Decision-making delays, coordination time |
| 6 | **Corporate L&D** | 2-6 months | Procurement cycles, multi-stakeholder approval |

### CHANNEL DEEP-DIVE:

#### A. Direct to Individual (LinkedIn + Email)
- **LinkedIn Ads**: $150-400/lead (B2B training), 5.58-12 CPC
- **LinkedIn Organic DMs**: 9.36% response rate with personalization, 5.44% without
- **Email (existing list)**: 36-42% open rate, 2-4% CTR (B2B benchmarks)

**FASTEST PATH**: Email existing contacts → LinkedIn DMs to warm network → LinkedIn ads

#### B. Corporate L&D (B2B Sales)
- **Sales Cycle**: 60-120 days average (3-6 months mid-market, 9-18 months enterprise)
- **Decision Makers**: 3-7 people in buying committee
- **Budget Cycle**: Often tied to fiscal year (Jan/Feb or June/July approvals)

**VERDICT**: Too slow for 2-week launch. Reserve for Q2 pipeline.

#### C. Partnerships (Chambers, Associations)
- **Pros**: Instant credibility, warm audience, low/no ad spend
- **Cons**: 1-3 week coordination time, revenue share expectations (20-40%)
- **Best For**: Filling 10-20 seats quickly in local market

#### D. Existing Dale Carnegie Customer Base
- **Gold Standard**: Pre-qualified, know the methodology, trust the brand
- **Challenge**: Access depends on franchise agreement/non-compete terms
- **Workaround**: Target "Dale Carnegie graduates" who want refresher/advanced content

## 3. PILOT LAUNCH STRATEGY

### FIRST CITY SELECTION:

**CRITERIA**: Where can you win fastest?
1. **Your home city** (network effect, no travel costs, local credibility)
2. **Existing client concentration** (if you have corporate clients there)
3. **Partner/association presence** (chamber willing to promote)
4. **Market size**: 500K-2M metro population (big enough for pipeline, small enough to dominate)

**RECOMMENDATION**: Start where you live. Network effects beat everything in a 2-week sprint.

### MINIMUM VIABLE MARKETING SPEND:

| Item | Cost | Purpose |
|------|------|---------|
| Event venue (4 hrs) | $0-500 | Library, co-working space, partner location |
| Landing page (Carrd/ConvertKit) | $0-29 | Registrations, payments |
| LinkedIn Ads (test budget) | $300-500 | 3-5 leads if optimized |
| Email tool (Mailchimp/ConvertKit) | $0-50 | Existing list outreach |
| Coffee/meeting costs | $100 | Partner meetings |
| **TOTAL MVP** | **$400-1,200** | |

### QUICK-WIN TACTICS (What Works Fast):

1. **"Founding Members" Pricing** — First 10 seats at 50% off (creates urgency)
2. **Bring-a-Friend BOGO** — 2-for-1 drives word-of-mouth
3. **Free Preview Webinar** — 30-min teaser 1 week before (builds list, converts to paid)
4. **LinkedIn Personal Posts** — "I'm hosting..." (authentic > corporate)
5. **Direct Invite Voice Notes** — 30-sec voice DMs (response rate 2-3x text)

### BETA PRICING STRATEGY:

**Dale Carnegie Benchmark**: $284-$2,500 per program (flagship: $2,395)

**Recommended Beta Pricing**:
- **Early Bird** (first 10): $497 (60% off market rate) — "Founding Member"
- **Standard**: $997 (still 50% below DC flagship)
- **VIP** (1-on-1 follow-up): $1,497

**Why this works**: Positions as premium but accessible; DC grads see value; creates upgrade path

## 6. WHAT COULD GO WRONG?

### CRITICAL RISKS:

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|--------|------------|
| Low registration (cold start) | HIGH | Launch delay | Start with free webinar, build list first |
| Partner delays | MEDIUM | 50% fewer leads | Have 3+ partners in pipeline, don't rely on 1 |
| Ad account issues | LOW | Lost paid channel | Don't rely on ads for 2-week launch |
| Venue cancellation | LOW | Event failure | Have backup location, confirm 48hrs ahead |
| Low attendance | MEDIUM | Poor experience | Over-register (expect 40% no-show), create waitlist |

### REALISTIC TIMELINE ADJUSTMENTS:

**If you have NONE of these pre-built assets**:
- Existing email list (500+)
- Warm LinkedIn network (active connections)
- Partner relationships
- Past attendee base

**THEN**: 2-week launch is NOT RECOMMENDED. Instead:
- Week 1-2: Build list with free webinar or lead magnet
- Week 3-4: Promote paid program to new list
- Week 5-6: Deliver program

**REALISTIC MINIMUM**: 4-6 weeks from cold start to filled seats

## 7. LONG-TERM SCALING STRATEGY

### MULTI-CITY TOUR LOGISTICS:

**Phase 1 (Months 1-3)**: Home city only — prove model, get testimonials
**Phase 2 (Months 4-6)**: 2nd city with partner — test replication
**Phase 3 (Months 7-12)**: 4-6 cities with consistent partners

**Per-City Requirements**:
- Local partner (chamber, association, or training company)
- Venue (hotel, co-working space, partner facility)
- Marketing lead time: 4-6 weeks per city
- Travel costs: $500-1,500 per trip

### VIRTUAL DELIVERY PIVOT:

**When**: After 3-6 in-person pilots prove content
**Benefits**: 
- No venue costs
- Larger geography (national/international)
- Higher margins
- Easier to scale

**Challenges**:
- Lower engagement than in-person
- More competition
- Different marketing (SEO/content becomes viable)

### LICENSING TO OTHER TRAINERS:

**Model**: Train-the-trainer certification
**Timeline**: 12-18 months (after curriculum proven)
**Revenue**: $5K-15K per licensee + revenue share

### CORPORATE TRAINING CONTRACTS:

**Entry Path**: Convert individual attendees into corporate champions
**Cycle**: 3-6 months (nurture attendees → L&D introduction → proposal)
**Deal Size**: $10K-100K+ per contract
**Key**: Build case studies from public workshops first
