# Meta (Facebook/Instagram) Advertising Strategy for Dale Carnegie Training Programs

**Prepared for:** Ontario B2B Market  
**Date:** February 2026  
**Focus:** Lead Generation for Corporate Training Programs

---

## Executive Summary

This strategy leverages Meta's 2025-2026 advertising capabilities to reach Ontario B2B decision-makers for Dale Carnegie training programs. With 49% of B2B decision-makers using Facebook for research and broad targeting paired with first-party signals cutting CPA by 32%, Meta presents a high-ROI channel when properly structured. The recommended approach combines sophisticated audience layering, multi-format creative testing, and a structured funnel that nurtures leads from initial awareness through program enrollment.

---

## 1. Targeting Options for Ontario B2B Decision Makers

### Geographic Targeting
- **Primary Location:** Ontario, Canada
  - Include: Toronto, Ottawa, Mississauga, Hamilton, London, Kitchener, Windsor, Markham, Vaughan
  - Exclude: Quebec border regions (to improve precision)
- **Radius Targeting:** 25km around major business districts for in-person workshop promotion

### Core Audience: Business Decision Makers

Meta's **Business Decision-Maker Titles and Interests** audience is the foundational targeting layer. This segment targets people based on job titles and professional interests indicating purchasing authority.

**Primary Job Title Targeting:**
| Title Category | Specific Titles to Target |
|----------------|---------------------------|
| C-Suite | CEO, COO, CFO, CTO, CMO, CHRO |
| VP/Director | VP of Operations, VP of HR, Director of Training, Director of Talent Development |
| Senior Management | General Manager, Regional Manager, Business Development Manager |
| Training & HR | HR Manager, Training Manager, Learning & Development Manager |

### Interest-Based Layering

Layer decision-maker titles with relevant interests to expand reach while maintaining relevance:

- **Professional Development:** Leadership Development, Business Coaching, Professional Coaching, Management Training
- **Human Resources:** Human Resources, Talent Management, Recruiting, Employee Engagement
- **Business:** Entrepreneurship, Small Business, Corporate Finance, Business Development
- **Industry Associations:** SHRM (Society for Human Resource Management), ATD (Association for Talent Development), Canadian Management Centre

### Custom Audiences (First-Party Data)

Build these high-value custom audiences:

1. **Website Visitors** – Pixel data from training program pages, webinar registrations, workshop landing pages
2. **Email List** – Existing leads and past program participants (upload customer lists)
3. **Engagement Audiences** – People who engaged with your content (video views >50%, post reactions, page followers)
4. **Lead Form Respondents** – Those who started but didn't complete lead forms

### Lookalike Audiences

Create 1% and 3% lookalikes from:
- Past program participants (best quality)
- High-value lead list (converted to sales)
- Email list subscribers

### Recommended Targeting Stack

```
Layer 1: Job Titles (Decision Makers)
    +
Layer 2: Interests (Leadership, HR, Training, Business)
    +
Layer 3: Location (Ontario, Canada)
    +
Optional Layer 4: Exclude past program participants
```

**Pro Tip:** Broad targeting with Advantage+ campaigns can reduce CPA by 32% when paired with first-party signals (website visitors, customer lists). Test broad targeting with interest signals against precise title targeting.

## 2. Ad Creative Recommendations

### Format Strategy by Funnel Stage

| Funnel Stage | Recommended Format | Purpose |
|--------------|-------------------|---------|
| Awareness | Short-form video (15-30 sec) | Spark curiosity, build brand awareness |
| Interest/Consideration | Carousel, Lead Ads | Educate, demonstrate value |
| Decision | Lead Ads, Single Image with CTA | Capture leads, drive registrations |
| Conversion | Retargeting video, testimonials | Nurture to close |

### Video Ads (Primary for TOFU)

**Why Video Works for B2B:**
- 62% of users report interest after seeing a video on Facebook
- Higher engagement rates than static formats
- Builds trust and demonstrates expertise

**Video Creative Recommendations:**

1. **Testimonial Videos (Highest Impact)**
   - 30-60 seconds
   - Real Ontario/Canadian business leaders sharing transformation results
   - Include: Company context, specific outcome (e.g., "Our team communications improved by 40%")
   - Hook in first 3 seconds

2. **Quick Tips Series**
   - 15-second clips with leadership/communication tips
   - Format: "Did you know?" or "One thing great leaders do differently..."
   - End with subtle brand mention and CTA

3. **Behind-the-Scenes / Workshop Teasers**
   - Show workshop environment, facilitator in action
   - 20-30 seconds
   - Build credibility and reduce perceived risk

**Video Specs:**
- Aspect ratio: 9:16 (Stories/Reels) or 1:1 (Feed)
- First 3 seconds must hook
- Include captions (85% autoplay with sound off)
- End with clear CTA

### Carousel Ads (MOFU - Best for Education)

**Best Use Cases:**
- Highlight different program modules/outcomes
- Share client success stories across cards
- Present statistics and proof points

**Carousel Creative Structure:**

```
Card 1: Problem/Challenge (attention hook)
Card 2: Solution/Approach
Card 3: Result/Outcome (with metric)
Card 4: Social Proof (testimonial)
Card 5: CTA (Register for Webinar/Free Guide)
```

**Specs:**
- 10.8 x 10.8 px minimum (1:1 ratio)
- Up to 10 cards
- Each card needs independent headline and description
- Use consistent visual branding

### Single Image Ads (BOFU - Lead Capture)

**Best Use:**
- Lead generation ads (using Meta Lead Forms)
- Retargeting cold audiences with strong offer
- Event promotion

**Design Guidelines:**
- Clean, professional aesthetic
- Minimal text (use text overlay tool to check <20% text)
- Strong contrast
- Single clear value proposition
- Dominant CTA button

### Lead Ads (Critical for Ontario B2B)

Meta Lead Ads reduce friction by keeping users on-platform:

- Use **Lead Generation** objective
- Keep form fields to 3-5 max (name, email, company, phone, job title)
- Include qualifying questions to filter low-quality leads
- Enable "Apply now" or "Get your free guide" CTAs

## 3. Funnel Strategy: Lead Magnet → Webinar → Workshop → Program

### Funnel Architecture

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│                     TOP OF FUNNEL (TOFU)                       │
│  Objective: Brand Awareness + Lead Magnet Distribution         │
│  Ad Formats: Video Views, Traffic                              │
│  Lead Magnet: Free Guide, Assessment, Checklist                │
└──────────────────────────┬──────────────────────────────────────┘
                           │
                           ▼
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│                   MIDDLE OF FUNNEL (MOFU)                      │
│  Objective: Webinar/Event Registration                          │
│  Ad Formats: Lead Generation, Traffic, Video Views            │
│  Content: Educational Webinar on Leadership/Communication      │
└──────────────────────────┬──────────────────────────────────────┘
                           │
                           ▼
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│                 BOTTOM OF FUNNEL (BOFU)                        │
│  Objective: Workshop Enrollment / Program Sale                 │
│  Ad Formats: Lead Generation, Conversions                     │
│  Content: Workshop invitations, Program testimonials           │
└──────────────────────────┬──────────────────────────────────────┘
                           │
                           ▼
                    CUSTOMER ACQUISITION
```

### Stage 1: Lead Magnet (TOFU)

**Objective:** Capture contact information, build email list

**Offer Ideas:**
- "The Ontario Leaders Communication Guide" (PDF)
- "10 Communication Mistakes Killing Your Team's Performance" (Checklist)
- "Leadership Assessment: How Do You Score?" (Interactive tool)
- "Dale Carnegie Impact Calculator" (ROI tool)

**Ad Creative:**
- Short video (15 sec) teasing the guide
- Carousel highlighting what's inside
- Single image with strong hook

**Targeting:** Broad (Ontario), interest-based, lookalikes

**Expected CPL:** $15-35 CAD

### Stage 2: Webinar (MOFU)

**Objective:** Build trust, demonstrate expertise, warm leads

**Webinar Topics:**
- "Building High-Performance Teams Through Communication"
- "Leadership Presence in the Modern Workplace"
- "From Manager to Leader: The Dale Carnegie Transformation"
- "Conflict Resolution Strategies for Ontario Business Leaders"

**Ad Creative:**
- Video testimonials from past attendees
- Carousel showing webinar agenda/speakers
- Retargeting people who downloaded lead magnet

**Registration Flow:**
1. Lead Ad → Meta Form → Webinar Registration
2. Traffic Ad → Landing Page → Webinar Registration

**Expected CPL:** $25-50 CAD

### Stage 3: Workshop (BOFU)

**Objective:** Drive in-person/virtual workshop attendance

**Workshop Formats:**
- Half-day "Dale Carnegie Preview" workshop
- Full-day "Leadership Fundamentals" intensive
- Virtual "Dale Carnegie Live" sessions

**Ad Creative:**
- Video: Past workshop footage, participant testimonials
- Limited-time offer urgency
- Retargeting webinar attendees who didn't convert

**Expected CPL:** $40-80 CAD

### Stage 4: Program Enrollment

**Objective:** Close the sale

**Conversion Paths:**
1. Workshop → Program enrollment (natural progression)
2. Webinar → Sales call → Program
3. Direct program inquiry

**Ad Creative:**
- Program testimonials with results
- ROI/proof-focused content
- Limited-time pricing (if applicable)
- Retargeting warm audiences

**Expected CPL:** $60-150 CAD (higher value, lower volume)

## 4. Retargeting Best Practices

### Custom Audience Building

**Website Visitors (Pixel-Based):**
- All visitors (30-day window)
- Landing page visitors only (14-day window)
- Webinar registration page (don't complete) (7-day window)
- Pricing page visitors (30-day window)

**Engagement Audiences:**
- Video viewers (50%+ watched, 180-day window)
- Page engagers (last 180 days)
- Lead form openers (not submitters)
- Instagram followers

**Customer Lists:**
- Past program participants
- Email subscribers
- Sales pipeline leads

### Retargeting Segments

| Audience | Description | Ad Approach |
|----------|-------------|-------------|
| Lead Magnet Downloaders | Downloaded guide, no further action | Webinar invitation |
| Webinar Registrants (No-Show) | Registered, didn't attend | Recording + next step |
| Webinar Attendees | Attended live | Workshop invitation |
| Website Visitors (High Intent) | Visited pricing/program pages | Direct program CTA |
| Cart/Form Abandoners | Started lead form, didn't submit | Simplified form, offer |
| Cold Prospects | Broad audience, visited site | Brand awareness, differentiator |

### Retargeting Creative Strategy

**Sequential Retargeting:**

```
Ad 1: Problem Awareness (Video: 15 sec)
    ↓
Ad 2: Solution Presentation (Carousel: Your solution)
    ↓
Ad 3: Social Proof (Testimonial video)
    ↓
Ad 4: CTA (Lead form/Webinar registration)
```

**Frequency Management:**
- Review frequency weekly
- Pause ads when frequency exceeds 5 impressions per user
- High frequency + falling CTR = creative fatigue (refresh immediately)

### Retargeting Best Practices for B2B

1. **Use Advantage+ Shopping/Conversion campaigns** for automated optimization
2. **Combine custom audiences with broad targeting** for 4-7x ROAS improvement
3. **Set up Meta Conversions API** for better tracking accuracy
4. **Exclude past customers** from prospecting (unless upselling)
5. **Create separate ad sets for each audience segment** for precise optimization

## 5. Budget Allocation Recommendations

### Budget Framework (70/20/10 Model)

For a **$5,000/month** total budget:

| Funnel Stage | Allocation | Monthly Spend | Purpose |
|--------------|------------|---------------|---------|
| TOFU (Awareness) | 40% | $2,000 | Lead magnets, video views |
| MOFU (Consideration) | 30% | $1,500 | Webinar promotions |
| BOFU (Decision) | 30% | $1,500 | Workshop/program ads |

*Note: BOFU gets more budget relative to volume because these convert at higher rates and justify larger spend per lead.*

### Budget Optimization by Campaign Type

**Cold Prospecting (New Audiences):**
- 40-50% of total ad spend
- Use Advantage+ (formerly CBO) for automated optimization
- Test multiple creative variations

**Retargeting (Warm Audiences):**
- 30-40% of total ad spend
- Higher conversion rates justify investment
- Use manual bidding for control

**Lookalike Audiences:**
- 10-20% of total ad spend
- Scale winning audiences

### Daily Budget Minimums

| Campaign Type | Minimum Daily Budget |
|---------------|---------------------|
| Lead Generation | $20-35/day per ad set |
| Video Views | $10-15/day |
| Retargeting | $15-25/day |

**Rule of Thumb:** Minimum $10-20 per ad set per day to exit the learning phase quickly.

### Scaling Strategy

1. **Scale winners** by increasing budget 20-30% weekly
2. **Duplicate winning campaigns** with new audiences
3. **Use Campaign Budget Optimization** to let algorithm allocate
4. **Scale based on ROI**, not just impressions or clicks

### Monthly Budget Progression (New Campaign)

**Month 1:** $3,000 - Testing phase, learn what works
**Month 2:** $5,000 - Scale winners, optimize losers
**Month 3:** $7,000+ - Full scale on proven creatives/audiences

## 6. Key Metrics to Track

### Funnel Metrics by Stage

| Stage | Metric | Target Benchmark | Notes |
|-------|--------|-----------------|-------|
| **TOFU** | CPM (Cost per 1,000 impressions) | $8-15 CAD | Industry varies |
| | Video Views (2 sec) | <$0.03 CAD | Lower is better |
| | Cost per Lead (CPL) | $15-35 CAD | Depends on offer |
| | Reach | 50,000-100,000/mo | Scale over time |
| **MOFU** | Webinar Registrations | $25-50 CAD | Per registration |
| | Registration Rate | 3-8% | From ad impression |
| | Webinar Attendance Rate | 40-60% | Of registered |
| **BOFU** | Workshop Registrations | $40-80 CAD | Per registration |
| | Cost per Enrolled Student | $60-150 CAD | Program enrollment |
| | Program Close Rate | 15-25% | From workshop attendees |

### Platform KPIs

| Metric | Definition | Target |
|--------|------------|--------|
| **CTR (Click-Through Rate)** | Clicks / Impressions | 1.4-1.7% (B2B benchmark) |
| **CPC (Cost per Click)** | Ad Spend / Clicks | $0.50-2.00 CAD |
| **CPL (Cost per Lead)** | Ad Spend / Leads | $20-60 CAD (weighted average) |
| **Conversion Rate** | Leads / Website Visits | 3-8% |
| **Frequency** | Avg impressions per user | 2-4 (ideal), >5 (fatigue) |
| **ROAS** | Revenue / Ad Spend | 3-5x minimum for sustainability |

### Attribution & Tracking

**Required Tracking Stack:**
1. Meta Pixel (base tracking)
2. Meta Conversions API (server-side, for accuracy)
3. UTM parameters on all links
4. Offline conversion tracking (program enrollments)

**Key Attribution Windows:**
- View-through: 1-day
- Click-through: 7-day
- Track full customer journey from first touch to sale

### Monthly Optimization Decisions

| Metric | Action |
|--------|--------|
| CPL above target | Refresh creative, narrow audience, review landing page |
| CTR below 1% | New creative hook, test video |
| Frequency >5 | Pause or rotate ads |
| ROAS <2x | Review audience quality, increase retargeting spend |
| Good ROAS >4x | Scale budget 20-30% |
