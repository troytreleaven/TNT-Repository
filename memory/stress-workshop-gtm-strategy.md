## Executive Summary

This document outlines a comprehensive Go-To-Market (GTM) strategy for a high-impact workshop campaign designed to drive pipeline growth for Dale Carnegie Training's flagship "Lead with Impact" program. The campaign centers on a compelling, timely offer: **"Lead Through Stress & Uncertainty"** — a strategic workshop addressing the most pressing challenge facing leaders today.

### Strategic Overview

| Element | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| **Campaign Objective** | Build qualified pipeline that converts to Lead with Impact enrollments |
| **Lead Offer** | 90-minute virtual workshop: "Lead Through Stress & Uncertainty" |
| **Price Point** | Free or $49 (early bird) — lead generation focus |
| **Upsell Product** | Lead with Impact (3-Day Boot Camp + Leadership Labs) — $2,495+ |
| **Target Market** | Ontario & Maritimes (Canada) — mid-to-large enterprises |
| **Primary Funnel** | Meta Ads → Workshop Registration → Nurture → Lead with Impact Enrollment |

### Key Projections (Conservative Scenario - $1,000/month)

- **Monthly Leads:** 67-125 (CPL: $8-15)
- **Workshop Registrants:** 20-40 (30-40% conversion)
- **Workshop Attendees:** 10-20 (50% show rate)
- **SQLs Generated:** 4-8 (20-40% of attendees)
- **Lead with Impact Enrollments:** 1-2 (15-25% SQL close rate)
- **Monthly Revenue:** $2,495 - $4,990
- **ROI (Monthly):** 83% - 166%

### Recommendation

Launch with a **$1,000/month Meta ad budget** to validate the funnel before scaling. The projected return justifies immediate investment, with clear expansion paths at $2,000/month as conversion rates prove out.

```
DAY 0: Workshop Delivery
    │
    ├─→ Post-workshop survey (immediate)
    │       ↓
    │   Survey response → HOT LEAD FLAG
    │
    ├─→ Thank you email + Recording (same day)
    │       ↓

    │
    ├─→ Nurture Email Sequence (Days 1-7)
    │   - Day 1: Resources + consultation offer

    │   - Day 5: Program overview + early bird
```

### 6.2 Timeline: Workshop to Enrollment

| Phase | Timeline | Activities |
|-------|----------|------------|

**Optimal Close Rate Window:**
- 60% of enrollments close within 14 days post-workshop

## 8. Risk Factors and Mitigation

### 8.1 Identified Risks

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|-------------|
| **Low workshop attendance** | Medium | High | Multi-channel reminders, reminder calls, recorded option |
| **Poor lead quality** | Medium | Medium | Refine targeting, improve lead magnet, stricter qualification |
| **Low conversion to sales** | Medium | High | Sales training, better follow-up scripts, shorter sales cycle |
| **Ad fatigue** | Medium | Medium | Rotate creatives, expand audiences, test new placements |
| **Competition from free content** | High | Low | Focus on Dale Carnegie brand, unique methodology, community |
| **Budget constraints** | Low | High | Start lean, validate before scaling, prioritize high-ROI activities |
| **Delivery capacity issues** | Low | Medium | Train backup facilitators, record workshops for scale |

### 8.2 Contingency Plans

| Scenario | Response |
|----------|----------|
| CPL > $20 | Pause, optimize creative/audience, test landing page variations |
| Attendance < 10 | Increase promotion, add in-person option, improve reminder sequence |
| Sales cycle > 30 days | Simplify offer, add payment plans, create urgency |
| Enrollment < 1/month | Revisit pricing, enhance value proposition, add bonuses |
