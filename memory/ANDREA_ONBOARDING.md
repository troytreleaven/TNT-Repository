# Andrea 90-Day Onboarding Plan
## Source: Shared by Troy on Feb 22, 2026

## Program Requirements (Must Complete)
- Dale Carnegie Course - within a few weeks of hire
- Winning with Relationship Selling - within 60 days
- Leadership Training for Managers - within 120 days
- Sales Best Practices Training - after first 30 days

## Competency Milestones

### By Day 30
- Barista Challenge and Coffee Track
- 3-4 Core products
- Graduate Conversation
- Decision Maker Call from Grad Individual
- CIS - then call to DM for teams
- Cycle of Self-Development
- Triangle of Learning
- Inbound Lead
- Networking
- Seminar/Preview Follow-up and Conversion
- Client Credibility Statements
- 5 USPs

### By Day 60
- All items from first 30 days
- Basic Agenda Call - Agenda Statement
- Basic Agenda Call - Three distinct areas
- Basic Agenda Call - Questioning Model
- Basic Agenda Call - Executive Summary
- Basic Agenda Call - Solution Step
- Basic Agenda Call - Close
- Positioning
- Group CIS or BID

### By Day 90
- All items from first 60 days
- Basic Agenda Call - can complete without supervision
- Deliver a Group CIS
- Organize and implement a BID
- Discussion Document (proposal)
- Customer continuum process

## Daily Activities (Recurring)
- 10 graduate conversations per day
- 20 Decision maker calls per day
- 2 dormant accounts per day
- Attend client appointments with Sales Manager
- Classroom exposure and networking events

## Client Touch Target
- 150 clients in KW/Hamilton/Burlington/Niagara over 6 months
- ~25 clients per month
- ~6 clients per week

## Weekly Structure (Onboarding)
- Week 1: Orientation, core products, practice
- Week 2: Prospecting ramp-up, 10 grad/20 DM daily
- Week 3: Activity building, client list creation
- Week 4: First revenue, 30-day competency check
- Weeks 5-8: Basic Agenda Call, CIS, positioning
- Weeks 9-12: Group CIS, BID, full execution
