# OpenClaw Use Case Mapping for Dale Carnegie Ontario

## Quick Wins (Month 1-2) — Highest Immediate ROI

### 1. **Subagents & Delegation** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
**Business Problem:** Automating admin work, Philippines team elevation
- Create dedicated subagents for: lead follow-up, scheduling, email responses, podcast production
- Philippines team (Joanna, Jen, Mye, Brenda, Vin) can delegate repetitive tasks to subagents
- Elevate team from admin → customer-facing by offloading grunt work

**Impact:** Frees 10-15 hrs/week of manual work; enables team to focus on revenue-generating activities

### 2. **Threaded Chats** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
**Business Problem:** Multi-threaded client conversations, podcast production tracking
- Separate threads for: each sales prospect, each active program, podcast episode production
- Keeps context organized across long conversations (DCC is 8 weeks!)

**Impact:** Better client experience, no context loss, faster follow-up

### 3. **Cron Jobs** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
**Business Problem:** Consistent lead follow-up, reminders, podcast scheduling
- Automated follow-up sequences for leads at 3-day, 7-day, 14-day intervals
- Reminder system for program milestones (kickoff, week 4, graduation)
- Weekly podcast publishing schedule automation

**Impact:** Never miss a follow-up, systematic nurture campaigns

## Medium-Term (Month 2-4) — Building Infrastructure

### 4. **Prompt Optimizations** ⭐⭐⭐⭐
**Business Problem:** Consistent messaging, sales scripts, instructor onboarding
- Optimize prompts for: lead qualification, program explanation, objection handling
- Create reusable prompt templates for Philippines team

**Impact:** Consistent brand voice, faster onboarding, quality control

### 5. **Voice Memos** ⭐⭐⭐⭐
**Business Problem:** Podcast production, quick ideas capture
- Quick voice notes for podcast topics, guest ideas
- Dictate email responses, course feedback

**Impact:** Faster content creation, reduced friction for ideas

### 6. **Documentation/Backup** ⭐⭐⭐⭐
**Business Problem:** Knowledge retention, team continuity, Maritimes succession
- Document: sales playbooks, instructor guides, program delivery notes
- Critical for Paul Kearley succession (Aug 2026) — capture his knowledge

**Impact:** Business continuity, smooth transitions, institutional memory

### 7. **Model Routing** ⭐⭐⭐⭐
**Business Problem:** Cost optimization, different task requirements
- Route: simple queries → cheaper models, complex negotiations → premium
- Sales conversations get premium; admin tasks get efficient

**Impact:** 30-40% cost reduction on AI operations

## Long-Term (Month 4+) — Scale & Security

### 8. **Security Best Practices** ⭐⭐⭐⭐
**Business Problem:** Protecting client data, CRM access, payment information
- Implement proper access controls for Salesforce, payment data
- Secure credential management for team members

**Impact:** Risk mitigation, compliance, trust preservation

### 9. **Logging & Debugging** ⭐⭐⭐
**Business Problem:** Troubleshooting issues, tracking bot behavior
- Log agent actions for accountability and error investigation

**Impact:** Faster problem resolution, audit trail

### 10. **Agent-Native Hosting** ⭐⭐⭐
**Business Problem:** Custom infrastructure needs, data residency
- If sensitive data requires on-premise control

**Impact:** Compliance flexibility (rarely needed initially)

### 11. **Self Updating** ⭐⭐⭐
**Business Problem:** Keeping OpenClaw current without manual updates
- Automated updates for security patches, new features

**Impact:** Reduced maintenance, always current

### 12. **API vs Subscription** ⭐⭐⭐
**Business Problem:** Cost planning, scaling decisions
- Evaluate pay-per-use vs fixed costs as volume grows

**Impact:** Optimized spend at scale

### 13. **Testing** ⭐⭐⭐
**Business Problem:** Ensuring reliability before deployment
- Test prompts, workflows, integrations before full rollout

**Impact:** Fewer errors in production, smoother launches

## Summary

**Quick Wins (implement first):** Subagents, Cron Jobs, Threaded Chats
- These directly enable the sales team to focus on closing deals
- Automate the lead follow-up engine needed for $300K/month

**Foundation Builders (month 2-4):** Prompt Optimizations, Documentation, Voice Memos
- Quality control and knowledge capture
- Critical for Maritimes succession

**Scale Infrastructure (month 4+):** Security, Testing, Self-Updating
- Mature operations for larger volume

**Total dependency chain:** Subagents → Cron Jobs → Threaded Chats → Prompt Optimizations → Documentation
