## Event Format

### Half-Day Option (4 hours)
- Morning: Refresh key Dale Carnegie principles
- Mid: New tools and frameworks
- Afternoon: Action planning with peers

### Full-Day Option (6-7 hours)
- Morning: Deep dive into 1-2 principles
- Mid: New content delivery
- Afternoon: Business planning exercise
- Close: Commitments and follow-up

---

## Content Outline

1. **Reconnect** (30 min)
   - What did you learn in Dale Carnegie?
   - What's stuck? What's faded?

2. **Refresh** (60-90 min)
   - Top 3 principles revisited
   - Real-world applications

3. **New Tools** (60-90 min)
   - Fresh frameworks based on current challenges
   - Leadership under stress (leverage workshop content)
   - Change management refresher

4. **Action Planning** (60 min)
   - Where does your business need to improve?
   - Specific commitments

5. **Energy & Connection** (30-60 min)
   - Peer accountability partners
   - Networking
   - Next steps

## Target Audience

### Primary
- Past Dale Carnegie course graduates (DCC, HIP, etc.)
- 6-24 months post-course
- Companies with 10+ employees

### Secondary
- Past boot camp attendees
- Companies who bought team packages

### Exclusion
- People who took course >3 years ago (need re-enrollment, not refresh)

## Marketing & Outreach

- LinkedIn targeted ads by job title + course history

### Content
- "Remember that feeling after your Dale Carnegie course?"

### Follow-Up
- Day-of: Photo/content for social proof
- Day after: Thank you + resources + consultation offer
- 1 week: Check-in on action plans
