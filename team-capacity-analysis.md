## 6. BOTTLENECK ANALYSIS

### 6.1 PRIMARY CONSTRAINT: TRAINER AVAILABILITY

**The Paradox:** Sales can potentially drive $150K+/month, but delivery caps at ~$100K/month (conservative) or ~$160K/month (moderate).

**Why:**
- Victor is the workhorse (60%+ of delivery)
- Nicole split between sales and delivery
- Steve has limited availability
- Kathy not yet independent
- Grace only does HIP

**Solution Path:**
1. **Short-term:** Schedule confirmation, contract trainers on standby
2. **Medium-term:** Develop Kathy, add contract capacity
3. **Long-term:** Hire dedicated corporate trainer

### 6.2 SECONDARY CONSTRAINT: TROY'S TIME

Troy as the closer for large deals creates a bottleneck. Consider:
- Strategic account manager role
- Higher close rates for team (more training)
- Demo/video-based sales for mid-market
