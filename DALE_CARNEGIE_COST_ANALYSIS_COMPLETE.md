## 5. KEY INSIGHTS & RECOMMENDATIONS

### Critical Findings:

1. **Break-even is ~3 boot camps/month** at 16 participants each
   - Below 3 camps = losing money
   - At 3 camps = breaking even (~$2,834 profit)
   - Above 3 camps = profitable with strong margins

2. **Each additional boot camp above break-even adds ~$32,000/month to profit**
   - This is your contribution margin
   - Scaling from 3→6 camps = +$96,000/month profit

3. **Franchise fee (16.5% of revenue) is the largest single cost**
   - $297,000/year or $24,750/month
   - Represents 26.5% of all fixed costs

4. **Fixed costs are heavy at $93K/month**
   - Requires consistent volume to cover
   - 2 boot camps/month = $29K loss
   - Need reliable pipeline to sustain business

### Strategic Recommendations:

1. **Minimum Viable Volume:** Target 4+ boot camps/month consistently
   - Provides $34K+ monthly profit buffer
   - Covers fixed costs with room for variability

2. **Optimal Volume:** 6-8 boot camps/month
   - $99K-$163K monthly profit
   - Strong cash flow for growth/investments
   - Allows for team expansion and marketing

3. **Capacity Planning:** With current infrastructure
   - 12 boot camps/month = nearly $3.5M annual profit
   - Consider what's realistic given trainer capacity, room availability

4. **Cost Management:**
   - Franchise fee is non-negotiable but revenue-share model
   - Focus on variable cost efficiency (trainer fees, commissions)
   - Monitor credit card/room costs - $10K/month is significant
