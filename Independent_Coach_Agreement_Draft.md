# INDEPENDENT COACH AGREEMENT
## Dale Carnegie Business Group & Independent Coach

**Effective Date:** [DATE]  
**Engagement:** Ontario Massage Therapist Association Project

---

## 1. PARTIES & PURPOSE

**Dale Carnegie Business Group ("DC")** engages **[COACH NAME]** ("Coach") as an independent contractor to provide professional coaching services to DC's client, the Ontario Massage Therapist Association ("OMTA").

This Agreement establishes a **partnership framework** that protects DC's business relationships while respecting Coach's independent status.

---

## 2. INDEPENDENT CONTRACTOR STATUS

### 2.1 Relationship
Coach is an **independent contractor**, not an employee, agent, or partner of DC. Coach:
- Controls the manner, means, and methods of delivering services
- Is responsible for their own taxes, insurance, and business expenses
- May perform services for other clients (subject to Section 5)
- Is not entitled to employee benefits (vacation, EI, CPP contributions from DC)

### 2.2 No Exclusivity
This Agreement does not create an exclusive relationship. Coach may work with other training firms and clients, provided they comply with confidentiality and non-solicitation obligations in Sections 4 and 5.

## 4. CONFIDENTIALITY & INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

### 4.1 Definition of Confidential Information
"Confidential Information" means:
- **Client Information**: OMTA's business plans, employee information, organizational data not publicly available
- **Contact Lists**: OMTA personnel contact information obtained through this engagement
- **DC Proprietary Content**: Specific curriculum materials marked as proprietary, pricing structures, internal business strategies
- **Engagement Details**: Fee structures, contract terms, and commercial arrangements between DC and OMTA

*Excluded*: Information publicly available, already known to Coach before engagement, or independently developed without use of Confidential Information.

### 4.2 Confidentiality Obligations
Coach agrees to:
- Maintain Confidential Information in strict confidence
- Use Confidential Information solely for performing services under this Agreement
- Not disclose Confidential Information to third parties without DC's written consent
- Return or destroy all Confidential Information upon termination

**Duration**: 3 years from disclosure for general Confidential Information; indefinite for Contact Lists and trade secrets.

### 4.3 Intellectual Property
- **DC Materials**: All Dale Carnegie curriculum, trademarks, and proprietary methodologies remain DC's exclusive property
- **Work Product**: Materials created by Coach specifically for this engagement (customized exercises, assessments) are assigned to DC with full rights
- **Coach IP**: Coach retains ownership of pre-existing proprietary methods, published works, and general coaching frameworks not developed specifically for this engagement
- **Moral Rights**: Coach retains moral rights to their contributions; DC agrees to attribute authorship where appropriate

---

## 5. CLIENT RELATIONSHIP PROTECTION

### 5.1 DC's Client Relationship
DC maintains the master contractual relationship with OMTA. Coach acknowledges that:
- DC owns the business relationship with OMTA
- All billing flows through DC (see Section 6)
- DC manages contract renewals and expansions

### 5.2 Non-Solicitation (Limited)
For **12 months following termination** of this Agreement, Coach agrees not to:
- Directly solicit OMTA for services that compete with or substitute for services provided under this Agreement
- Use OMTA contact information obtained through this engagement to solicit OMTA personnel

**Exceptions**:
- General marketing not targeted at OMTA (e.g., LinkedIn posts, industry newsletters)
- OMTA personnel approaching Coach through independent channels (see Section 5.4)
- Services unrelated to training/coaching (e.g., consulting in Coach's other areas of expertise)

### 5.3 No Direct Engagement
During the term of this Agreement and for **12 months following termination**, Coach agrees:
- Not to contract directly with OMTA for services that compete with or substitute for services provided under this Agreement
- To refer all OMTA inquiries for Dale Carnegie-related services to DC
- Not to use OMTA contact information obtained through this engagement to market competing services

**Exceptions**:
- Services unrelated to training, coaching, or leadership development (e.g., consulting in Coach's other areas of expertise)
- General marketing not specifically targeted at OMTA (e.g., industry newsletters, public speaking)

### 5.4 Inbound Opportunities
If OMTA personnel approach Coach directly for Dale Carnegie-related services:
- Coach must refer the opportunity to DC within 48 hours
- DC retains exclusive right to quote, negotiate, and deliver all Dale Carnegie-related services to OMTA
- Coach may participate in service delivery as a subcontractor to DC if mutually agreed

## 6. COMPENSATION & BILLING

### 6.1 Fee Structure
DC will pay Coach:
- **Hourly Rate**: $____ per hour for coaching delivery
- **Per Diem**: $____ per day for full-day workshops
- **Expenses**: Pre-approved travel and materials reimbursed at cost

### 6.2 Payment Terms
- Coach submits invoice within 5 business days of service delivery
- DC pays within **30 days** of invoice receipt
- Late payments accrue interest at 1.5% per month (18% annually)

### 6.3 Billing Flow
DC bills OMTA directly and pays Coach as an independent contractor. Coach:
- Does not bill OMTA directly (unless exercising revenue-sharing option in Section 5.3)
- Receives 1099 (US) or T4A (Canada) for tax reporting
- Is responsible for all income tax, HST/GST, and other statutory obligations

---

## 7. INSURANCE & LIABILITY

### 7.1 Insurance Requirements
Coach must maintain:
- **Professional Liability Insurance (E&O)**: Minimum $2,000,000 per occurrence
- **General Liability Insurance**: Minimum $1,000,000 per occurrence
- DC named as **additional insured** on both policies
- Certificates of insurance provided before work commencement

### 7.2 Indemnification
**Coach indemnifies DC** for:
- Claims arising from Coach's negligence, misconduct, or breach of this Agreement
- Copyright/trademark infringement from materials Coach provides
- Defamation, discrimination, or harassment claims arising from Coach's conduct

**DC indemnifies Coach** for:
- Claims arising from DC's negligence or breach of this Agreement
- Claims arising from DC-provided materials or curriculum

### 7.3 Liability Cap
Each party's aggregate liability under this Agreement is **capped at fees paid in the 12 months preceding the claim**, except for:
- Indemnification obligations
- Confidentiality breaches
- Fraud or willful misconduct

## 8. TERM & TERMINATION

### 8.1 Term
This Agreement commences on [START DATE] and continues until [END DATE] or completion of the OMTA engagement, unless terminated earlier.

### 8.2 Termination for Convenience
Either party may terminate with **14 days' written notice** without cause.

### 8.3 Termination for Cause
Either party may terminate immediately for:
- Material breach of this Agreement (with 7-day cure period)
- Gross misconduct or negligence
- Criminal conduct or ethical violations

### 8.4 Post-Termination
Upon termination:
- Coach returns all DC materials and Confidential Information
- DC pays all outstanding fees for services rendered
- Non-solicitation obligations in Section 5 survive termination
- Confidentiality obligations in Section 4 survive termination

---

## 9. DISPUTE RESOLUTION

### 9.1 Good-Faith Negotiation
Parties will first attempt to resolve disputes through good-faith negotiation for 14 days.

### 9.2 Mediation
If unresolved, parties will engage in non-binding mediation with a mutually agreed mediator within 30 days.

### 9.3 Arbitration
If mediation fails, disputes will be resolved through **binding arbitration** under ADR Institute of Ontario rules:
- Venue: Toronto, Ontario
- Language: English
- Costs borne as determined by arbitrator

### 9.4 Governing Law
This Agreement is governed by the laws of **Ontario, Canada**. Parties submit to the exclusive jurisdiction of Ontario courts for injunctive relief.
