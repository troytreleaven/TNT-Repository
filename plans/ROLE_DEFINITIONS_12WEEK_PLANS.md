## SECTION 1.1: SALES REPRESENTATIVE ROLES

### Role: Sales Representative (Full-Time)
**Incumbents:** Manu Gill, Andrea Rodrigues

**Target:** $50,000/month revenue

---

#### Key Result Area 1: Revenue Generation

**Outcome:** Job is done well when the representative achieves monthly revenue target of $50,000 through a mix of Lead with Impact enrollments, DCC courses, HIP programs, and corporate training contracts.

**Activities & Duties:**
- Conduct outbound sales calls daily (minimum 25 calls/day)
- Send personalized follow-up emails (minimum 40 emails/day)
- Conduct discovery meetings with prospects
- Deliver presentations and proposals
- Negotiate and close deals
- Maintain pipeline of $100,000+ at all times
- Achieve 25% close rate on qualified opportunities

**Skills, Knowledge & Abilities:**
- Deep knowledge of Dale Carnegie methodology and programs
- Understanding of B2B sales cycle
- Strong negotiation skills
- Ability to present to C-suite executives
- Comfortable with CRM (Salesforce)
- Time management and prioritization

---

#### Key Result Area 2: Lead Management

**Outcome:** Job is done well when every lead is contacted within 4 hours and moved through the pipeline systematically, with accurate CRM data maintained at all times.

**Activities & Duties:**
- Respond to new leads within 4 hours
- Update CRM with call notes, next steps, and status after every interaction
- Schedule follow-up activities in CRM
- Qualify leads using BANT (Budget, Authority, Need, Timeline)
- Route leads to appropriate team members when necessary

**Skills, Knowledge & Abilities:**
- Proficiency in Salesforce or company CRM
- Strong organizational skills
- Attention to detail for data accuracy
- Understanding of lead qualification

---

#### Key Result Area 3: Territory/Account Development

**Outcome:** Job is done well when the representative builds strong relationships in their territory, resulting in repeat business and referrals.

**Activities & Duties:**
- Identify key accounts in territory
- Build relationships with decision-makers
- Conduct regular account reviews
- Identify expansion opportunities within existing accounts
- Generate referrals from satisfied customers
- Attend networking events in territory

**Skills, Knowledge & Abilities:**
- Relationship building skills
- Knowledge of territory companies
- Understanding of account management
- Networking abilities

---

#### Key Result Area 4: Pipeline Management

**Outcome:** Job is done well when the representative maintains a healthy pipeline that ensures consistent revenue flow, with opportunities progressing through stages on schedule.

**Activities & Duties:**
- Maintain pipeline value of minimum $100,000
- Move opportunities through stages weekly
- Identify and address stalled opportunities
- Forecast accurately for weekly meetings
- Conduct pipeline reviews with Sales Manager

**Skills, Knowledge & Abilities:**
- Forecasting skills

- Accurate data entry

#### Key Result Area 5: Product Knowledge

#### Key Result Area 6: Collaboration
