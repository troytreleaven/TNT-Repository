### Early Warning Signals

**What Gets Execs Out of Bed:**
1. Pipeline coverage drops below 2x monthly target
2. No new opportunities created in 7 days
3. Top 3 deals go stale (>30 days no progress)
4. Close rate drops below 25%
5. Average deal size declining

### Acceleration Tactics for Stuck Deals

1. **Identify the blocker:**
   - Budget? → Offer payment terms, smaller pilot
   - Timing? → Schedule specific future date, create urgency
   - Authority? → Elevate to executive sponsor
   - Competition? → Highlight unique differentiators

2. **Intervention Framework:**
   - Day 14: First follow-up (value add)
   - Day 30: Executive touch (not salesy)
   - Day 45: Either close or gracefully exit to nurture
